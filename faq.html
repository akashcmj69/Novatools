Frequently Asked Questions

Last Updated: October 09, 2025

What is Nova Tools?

Nova Tools is a platform offering a variety of free tools for PDF processing, financial calculations, image editing, and text utilities. Our goal is to provide user-friendly solutions to simplify your digital workflow.

Are the tools free to use?

Yes, all tools provided by Nova Tools are completely free to use. There are no hidden charges or subscriptions required.

How secure is my data when using Nova Tools?

We prioritize your privacy and security. All files uploaded to our platform are processed securely and deleted after use. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, you do not need to create an account. Our tools are accessible to everyone without requiring registration.

Can I use Nova Tools on my mobile device?

Yes, our website is fully responsive and optimized for use on both desktop and mobile devices. You can access all tools from your smartphone or tablet.

What types of tools does Nova Tools offer?

We offer a range of tools, including:

  • PDF Tools: Merge, split, convert, compress, and watermark PDFs.
  • Finance Tools: Calculate income tax, SIP, EMI, and GST.
  • Image Tools: Compress and resize images.
  • Utility Tools: Word counter, case converter, and remove duplicate lines.

How often are the tools updated?

We regularly update our tools to improve functionality and incorporate user feedback. Check our website or follow our updates for the latest features.

Can I suggest new tools or features?

What should I do if a tool is not working?

If a tool is not working as expected, try refreshing the page or using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact us via our Contact Us page, and we’ll assist you promptly.
