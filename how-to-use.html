How to Use the Nova Tools

Last Updated: October 09, 2025

1. Navigating the Website

The navigation bar at the top of the page allows you to access different sections of Nova Tools. Click on links like "Home," "Tools," "FAQ," "How to Use," or "About Us" to explore the website. The "Tools" link includes a dropdown menu with categories such as PDF Tools, Finance Tools, Image Tools, and Utilities, which you can access by hovering over it (or clicking on mobile devices).

2. Using the Theme Toggle

Nova Tools supports both dark and light themes for better visibility and comfort. To switch themes, click the theme toggle switch located in the top-right corner of the navigation bar. Your theme preference is saved, so the website will load in your chosen theme on future visits.

3. Accessing the Mobile Menu

On mobile devices, the navigation bar collapses into a hamburger menu icon (three horizontal lines) in the top-right corner. Tap the icon to open the menu, which slides in from the left. Tap the icon again or click the overlay to close the menu. You can also access the Tools dropdown by tapping the "Tools" link in the mobile menu.

4. Exploring Sections

The landing page is divided into sections, including a hero section with a welcome message and an "Explore All Tools" button. Clicking this button smoothly scrolls to the tools section. Each section (e.g., PDF Tools, Finance Tools) is accessible via the navigation bar or by scrolling down the page. Links in the navigation bar, such as "FAQ" or "About Us," take you to dedicated pages.

5. Visual Effects

The landing page features a particle background and a falling confetti effect for a dynamic experience. These effects adjust automatically based on your selected theme (dark or light) and do not require any interaction.

6. Footer Navigation

At the bottom of the page, the footer provides links to important pages like Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Contact Us, and Disclaimer. Click these links to learn more about our policies or to get in touch.

7. Contacting Support

If you have questions or encounter issues while navigating the landing page, visit our Contact Us
