How to Use the WhatsApp Chat to PDF Tool

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

1. Accessing the WhatsApp Chat to PDF Tool

Navigate to the Nova Tools homepage and locate the "WhatsApp Chat to PDF" tool card in the Tools section. Click the card, which displays a WhatsApp icon and the description "Convert your WhatsApp chat screenshot to a PDF document," to open the conversion modal.

2. Opening the Conversion Modal

After clicking the tool card, a modal titled "Convert WhatsApp Chat Screenshot to PDF" will appear. This modal contains an upload area for selecting WhatsApp chat screenshots. You can close the modal by clicking the "Ã—" button in the top-right corner or clicking outside the modal.

3. Selecting the Number of Images

Use the dropdown menu to select whether you want to upload 1 or 2 WhatsApp chat screenshots (`.png`, `.jpg`, or `.jpeg`). The default is 1 image. The upload hint will update to reflect your selection (e.g., "Select 1 WhatsApp chat screenshot(s) (.png, .jpg, .jpeg)").
