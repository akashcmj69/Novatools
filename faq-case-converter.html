Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Case Converter tool do?

The Case Converter on Nova Tools instantly transforms text into uppercase, lowercase, title case, or sentence case. It’s ideal for writers, editors, and anyone needing to adjust text formatting.

What types of text are supported?

The tool accepts any plain text input, including letters, numbers, and special characters, in any language. Simply type or paste your text into the textarea to convert its case.

How does each case conversion work?

Uppercase converts all letters to capitals (e.g., "hello" to "HELLO"). Lowercase converts to small letters (e.g., "HELLO" to "hello"). Title Case capitalizes the first letter of each word (e.g., "hello world" to "Hello World"). Sentence Case capitalizes the first letter of each sentence (e.g., "hello. world" to "Hello. World").

Who can benefit from this tool?

Writers, editors, students, and content creators use it to format text for documents, essays, or publications. For specific use cases or issues, reach out via our Contact Us page.
